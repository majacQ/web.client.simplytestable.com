Good afternoon! We’re Limegreentangerine - an independent creative design agency with over ten years experience of creating successful brand communication across all media…

We are a Cardiff based design agency specialising in branding, graphic design and web design. We provide companies with a competitive edge through the effective use of creative and strategic design. From individual start ups looking to realise their new vision to established organisations looking to enhance their market share; we use our knowledge of branding and understanding of the latest technology to promote organisations consistently across a multitude of ever changing touchpoints.

We work in both the private and public sector, for B2B and B2C markets. While each project is different the aims and objectives are often the same. We make these ambitions happen and sometimes even win awards on the way. It’s this variety that keeps us fresh, makes us hungry to achieve and gets us up in the morning!
